Images

Usage and implementation for Images within Web Design Language.

Introduction

Image use throughout the Web Design Language should adhere to the following types of imagery; Black and white pet imagery, article photography pack shots and user or pet avatars. Pet of pack imagery should not be used if the animal or pack is not legible.

Examples

Black and white pet imagery should only be used in areas of significance where a brand message it to be promoted such as a promotion or carousel panel. Black and white imagery should be used sparingley so as not to dilute the power of the image.

Article imagery should be used for introducing and highlighting the content of an article. Article imagery should be relevant to the article and should adhere to the Royal Canin Anthropomorphism views where animals should be respected for what they are and not portrayed with any human characteristics.
