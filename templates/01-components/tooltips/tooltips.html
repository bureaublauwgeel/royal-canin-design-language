Tooltips

Tooltips should be primarily to display additional content that is beneficial yet not crucial to the user.

Tabbed Tooltips are available but the content that is displayed in them should always primarily be presented in a way that does not requir user interactivity to reveal.

To put it simply, the main content should not rely on the Tooltip in order to be coherent.


A note on accessibiltiy

Since Tooltips are triggered by a hover, at this point there is some work to do in order to make them more accessibility friendly.

Similarly, whilst Tooltips work fine on touch based devices, they may not be the best way to present information as taps are required to trigger events instead of simply hovering.

For this reason it is worth stressing the point again, that only supplementary, non-critical content should live within a Tooltip.
